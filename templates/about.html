ABOUT S & S

Scales and Software (K) Ltd. is the trusted weighing solutions provider in Kenya. It is a company with a proven record of accomplishment in the supply of high quality products and service to a diverse clientele.

It has branches in Eldoret and Nairobi for easy reach of our customers and to give prompt and reliable services. The workshops are well equipped with advanced testing equipment and calibration services done. The stores are well stocked and customers expect and receive quality, durable equipment, delivered on time. Controls over spare parts are strict providing major components , including strain gauges, loadcells, digital indicators and weigh bars to guarantee quality standards.
